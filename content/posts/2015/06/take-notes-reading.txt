Here's a reminder why [making marks in books](/posts/making-proper-marks-in-books/) alone isn't such a good idea: they are hard to interpret. Margins are too narrow for notes. So write reading notes on index cards or in notebooks.

You might want to take a look at _[Reading -- Putting it all together](/posts/reading-putting-it-all-together/)_ to find out why highlights alone don't suffice for information retention.
