Using cookies

Our services are partly based on the use of cookies. These are used to make our services more user-friendly, effective and safe. A cookie is a small data file that is transmitted to your computer when you use our services. A cookie can only hold information that we have sent to your computer; private information cannot be retrieved from it. When you accept cookies on our website, we have no access to your personal information, but the cookies help us to identify your computer.

We mainly use session-specific cookies; they do not remain on your computer. When you leave our services, the temporary cookie is also discarded. We can use the compiled information to analyse the use patterns and structures of our website. In this way, we can continuously optimise our website by improving the content or personalisation and by making it easier to use.
