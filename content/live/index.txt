Once or twice a month on Thursday evenings, German time (UTC+1), we record a live video on our [YouTube page](http://www.youtube.com/c/ZettelkastenDe/live) and talk about implementing the Zettelkasten method, knowledge management in general, and how we use tools in our daily work for 30--60 minutes.

The benefit of "live": you can join us and participate in the comments!

We're open for guests on our show, too. If you want to join us, [get in touch](/legal/)!
